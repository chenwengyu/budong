Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters, collect power-ups, and evolve your character in this thrilling action game!

Game Features

Endless Adventure

Face waves of unique monsters in procedurally generated levels that ensure no two runs are the same.

Character Evolution

Collect power-ups and evolve your character with various abilities and upgrades.

Strategic Gameplay

Plan your moves and choose your upgrades wisely to survive longer and achieve higher scores.

How to Play

  • 1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map
  • 2. Automatically attack nearby enemies with your equipped weapons
  • 3. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities
  • 4. Survive as long as possible while defeating endless waves of monsters

Tips for Success

  • • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by enemies
  • • Choose complementary abilities that work well together
  • • Focus on collecting experience gems to level up quickly
  • • Pay attention to your health and try to avoid taking damage
